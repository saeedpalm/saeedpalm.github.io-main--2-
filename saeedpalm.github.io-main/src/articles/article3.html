Welcome to the pre-draft power rankings for the Mafia Fantasy League! As we gear up for another exciting season, let's take a look at where each team stands before the draft. These rankings are based on last season's performance and overall team potential. Without further ado, here are the rankings:

1. Mofag Fagiri

Last season's champion, looking for the first ever three peat.

2. Moutaz Sharif

Pretty hard to say good things about him, 2x champ politically has to be #2

3. Saeed Lutfi

Coming off the best regular season in league history, the commisioner has his eyes set on the price.

4. Abdel mansour

After reaching the playoffs for the first time the "OVO Owls" are looking to build on the moment of an Impressive performance last season.

5. Yassine Keyou

A high risk high reward player who isnt scared to make big moves, coming into his third season more comftorable than ever. Yassine is looking to put his IQ to use.

6. Ahmed Elghazali

After a super duper duper stinker of a season last year. The critics are back and they believe the luck has ran out for Ahmed Elghazali. But one bad season doesnt justify putting him at the bottom.

7. Salah Idris

Had a rough season last year, but potential for a strong comeback. A legacy season for Salah who has been fighting bad fortune for quite a while now.

8. Yasir Basbar

After making the playoffs for the first time Yb is looking to silence the critics and build a playoff streak. Lets just pray the yocan doesnt take over

9. Ali Lutfi

Trades trades trades. Not the highest iq in the league. Maybe hopping on the mofag trend will be the solution.

10. Skittles

Should be higher on the list. But with his recent lifestyle changes we dont think his mentality is a winning one.

11. Mj Yusufu

New management, fresh strategies, and a lot to prove. Only one rookie has made the playoffs in their first year. Will the pressure be too much?

12. Mubarak Ibrahim

The only player who hasnt dropped his balls yet and have a postive season. Some say he just suck, but he says its his luck. The media says he's Underdog team, with a lot of heart and determination.

Stay tuned for more updates as we approach the draft day. Who will rise to the top and who will fall? Only time will tell!
